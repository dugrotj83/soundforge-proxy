Process
HOW IT WORKS
From idea to finished track in under a minute.
1
DESCRIBE
Type your idea in plain language. Genre, mood, tempo, instruments, language — be as vague or specific as you want.
2
STRUCTURE
Optionally use metatags to define song structure: verses, chorus, bridge, specific vocal styles and instruments.
3
GENERATE
Our AI composes, performs, and produces your track in real time. Full stereo audio output in MP3 format.
4
DOWNLOAD
Preview your track instantly, then download the full audio file. Iterate with variations or tweaked prompts.
